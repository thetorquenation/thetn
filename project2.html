Ducati Streetfighter V4SP

The Torque Magazine

The minimalist full LED headlight perfectly captures the spirit of the Streetfighter V4. Though reminiscent of the front end of the Panigale V4, it also resembles the crazy grin of the Joker, the comic book character who inspired the designer responsible for creating the new Borgo Panigale naked in the Ducati Style Center. The full LED headlight assembly, part of the dual layer fairing, has a particular design that allows it to incorporate the air-box and LED cooling vents, while a rear deflector prevents flow from impacting directly on the tank. The headlight is surmounted by a 5” TFT display, the same as that of the Panigale V4, complete with sophisticated front cover that elegantly conceals attachments and wiring.
